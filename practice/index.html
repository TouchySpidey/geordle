G E O R D L E

How To Play

GEORDLE is a spin-off on the popular WORDLE game for Geography Lovers. This is the Practice Mode. Switch to Daily Mode for the actual Game.

Try names of COUNTRIES or CAPITALS depending on the Game Mode. This is a random selection as it is important to know CAPITALS too :).

Guess the COUNTRY/CAPITAL within the allowed number of tries. Hit the enter button to submit.

Lengths of your guesses does not matter, as long as it is a valid COUNTRY/CAPITAL (depending on the Game Mode).

Use the Hint button, to reveal ONE letter. There is a 5 sec time penalty for each use.

After each guess, the colour of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the answer.

Examples

D O D O M A



The letter 'D' is in the Answer, and in the right spot.

B A N G U I



The letter 'A' is in the Answer, but in the wrong spot.

A S M A R A



None of these letters are in the Answer at all.

Practice multiple times a day!
